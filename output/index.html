Inspiration through stories

This series of illustrated comics follows Ada as she embarks on adventures that explore what it means to do science and be a scientist. Each comic combines engaging storytelling with accurate scientific explanations, making complex ideas accessible and fun for children and educators alike.

Inclusive

The stories emphasize that anyone, regardless of identity or background, can pursue science if they are curious.

Accessible

Illustrated in black-and-white for classroom printing and distribution. They are available in 19 languages and are open source.

Educational

A selection of resources to bring Ada alive in your classroom.
