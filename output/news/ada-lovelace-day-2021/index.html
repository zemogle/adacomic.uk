Ada Lovelace Day 2021

  • Oct 05, 2021

We are pleased to announce our annual competition to celebrate Ada Lovelace day 2021, on 12 October 2021. We are offering 30 schools packs to UK-based educators*. You just need to send in your details and we will randomly pull 30 entries out of the (virtual) hat.

How to enter

To enter the competition, add your postal details in our Competition Entry Form.

This is open to UK teachers, parents of school-age children, home schoolers, and other educators.

Closing date: 26 October 2021

Good Luck!
