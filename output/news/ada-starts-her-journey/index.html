Ada starts her journey

  • May 01, 2018

Today Edward posted out all the individual comic books to our amazing Kickstarter backers. 41 went around UK, 15 to the rest of the European Union, 32 to USA, 4 to Canada, 1 to Singapore, 1 to Japan, 1 to Australia, and 1 to Argentina - 92 in total!

The lovely people in my small post office (inside Cardiff University Students' Union) were very patient and helped to put on all the international stamps. Alice, our intern who is helping with the shipping, put all the UK stamps - AND did all the packing.
